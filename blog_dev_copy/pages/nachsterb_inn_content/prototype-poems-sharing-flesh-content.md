regard, a house of trees--
each, un.der.ground, an ancestry;
or, autoerotic requiem.
now and then,
(pretend) they
might gaze across the aisle
and see another.
profligate panegyric
or rabble only
can they call
a peer.
laurel and redress appear.
no citizens here.



a play of string
lace?
a clasp?
thread count and flesh
interlace,
hands clasp.
a pluck, a coo,
a pitch and fall
struck a coup
when two stood tall.
