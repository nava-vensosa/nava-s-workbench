## Layer Breakdown

### Layer 1: Background (0-30% opacity)
- **Torus topology**: Wireframe rotating slowly
- **Color**: Muted blue (#1a4d6d)
- **Effect**: Subtle, atmospheric

### Layer 2: Vector Field (30-50% opacity)
- **Quaternion rotations**: Arrows showing rotation
- **Color gradient**: Purple â†’ Blue â†’ Yellow
- **Size variation**: Based on field magnitude
- **Animation**: Full rotation every 20 seconds

### Layer 3: Parametric Curves (50-70% opacity)
- **Hopf fibration**: Two interweaving paths
- **Colors**: Purple (#8338ec) and Blue (#3a86ff)
- **Glow**: Moderate intensity (0.4-0.6)
- **Animation**: Phase rotation every 8-10 seconds

### Layer 4: Wave Functions (70-90% opacity)
- **Three waves**: Overlapping interference
- **Colors**: Cyan family (#06ffa5, #48cae4, #90e0ef)
- **Glow**: Strong (0.5-0.8)
- **Animation**: Oscillating phases

### Layer 5: Particles (20-50% opacity)
- **Count**: 300 particles total
- **Motion**: Following vector fields
- **Size**: 2-4 pixels
- **Color**: White and purple

### Layer 6: Typography (100% opacity)
- **ITP/IMA**: 120pt, white, center top
- **WINTER SHOW**: 56pt, cyan, below title
- **2025**: 96pt, purple, below subtitle
- **Details**: 24-32pt, various colors, bottom

### Layer 7: Grid & Accents (10% opacity)
- **Grid**: 100px spacing
- **Accent lines**: Left and right edges
- **Technical details**: Bottom corners
